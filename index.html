How to Play

Cell confluency is the percentage of a culture dish's surface area covered by cells. Scientists check it before every experiment — it affects cell behavior and experimental results.

Each day you get a new stylized image of a HEK293 cell culture at a random confluency. Use the slider to estimate the coverage percentage, then submit.

Scoring — based on how close your guess is:
